The hardest areas to count in Washington

Census officials analyzed likely participation based on how many people in each area responded during the 2010 census and a range of more recent demographic information, including foreign-born populations, race, ethnicity and number of renters. The darker the color in the map below, the more difficult to count that area is expected to be.
